Reflux provides you platform to exhibit your project, model or experiment to demonstrate the advancements in Chemical Engineering
Prizes worth 15000/- and participation certificate to be given. So hurry up and register yourself for Reflux Exhibition.

Past exhibitions

Exhibitions at Reflux have always maintained levels that supersede other scientific exhibitions and Previous editions of Reflux witnessed the display of models ranging from solar energy devices like solar-steel to chemical devices like fuel cells, Chem-E-Car, biological applications like tissue culture to eco-friendly practices like vermicom- posting, and to quote a few moreexamples biological fuel-cell, biogas generation unit etc.

Participate
  • All students (UG/PG) are eligible to take part in this event
  • A maximum of 3 members can form a team
  • Submit the details of your model along with a brief description in word/PDF format
  • Showcase your model during Reflux in front of experienced panel of judges
  • Participation certificate will be given to all teams
  • Prizes worth 15000/- will be given to the best entries
