Process Design Problem
About
The steps of the engineering design process are to define a problem, do background research, specify requirements, optimize the solution, build a prototype and then finally go for testing and follow the loop again (if estimation was not wise). Here we pose a challenge before you to come up with an optimized and economic solution to a problem related to Process and Refinery industry applying your Chemical Engineering fundamentals. Please read the section below to know the details and participate.
