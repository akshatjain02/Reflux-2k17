Rural Tech
About
Rural Tech is a theme based event that provides participants a platform to present their ideas based on a problem that shall be pre-provided based on what the people of our country rural side suffer. You shall have to think of an idea that can help in solving that particular problem with usage of materials easily available in rural areas or processes that can easily be set up or have been used quite often. The aim of this competition is to solve the problem that people have readily faced and yet for generations, no one came up with the solution to that problem in an efficient manner.
