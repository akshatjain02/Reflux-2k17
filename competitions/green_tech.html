About

Are you the one who just talk of doing something good to maintain the environment or actually does something?

Green Tech is a competition to involve people in Sustainable Development and to bring awareness among them about the effects of neglecting the environmental issues.

Reflux gives you a platform to exhibit your knowledge and innovation with the latest eco-friendly technologies for making the campuses across India more sustainable and green, and promote the idea and the necessity of the same among the college going youth of this country.

Registrations for Green Tech are open!

Please read the sections below to know the details and participate.

Details

  • A team can have a maximum of 2 members
  • The event will consist of 2 rounds
  • Teams have to submit an abstract based on the Problem statement
  • Top teams based on the abstract will be invited to IIT Guwahati for the Onsite round during Reflux(25-27th March)
