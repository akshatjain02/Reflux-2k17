About

Reflux announces the photography competition for all the photography enthusiasts to showcase their Photography skills.

Participants have to send thier entries on the theme of Reflux 2016 that is, 'Climate Unchange: For a better tomorrow' along with an appropriate caption describing the entry.

Lots of amazing prizes to be won!!!

Submissions are open!

Please read the section below to know the details and participate.

Rules

Team participation is not allowed

  • A participant can submit a maximum of two entries
  • Your Photo should very well depict our competition theme "Climate Unchange: For a better tomorrow"
  • Size of the pictures should not be less than 2048*1536 pixels; this is close to 3.2 mega pixels
  • Do not include credentials such as name/address etc. on the photographs
  • Watermarks are not allowed
  • Digital manipulation that distorts the reality of the photos will not be allowed.
  • Only basic enhancements such as sharpening, contrast adjustment, or simple cropping will be allowed.

Submission

  • Make your submission through the below given link.
  • You need to upload your image along with a caption and short description.
  • Your photos will uploaded on our FB page
  • Judging criteria: 50% based on Facebook Likes & rest on Judges
  • Tagging, Sharing of photos is allowed

Note :
Submissions for this competition can be made upto 21th March *
All submitted photographs will be the property of Reflux
In case of any discrepancies, the final rights are reserved with Reflux
