About
The earth’s climate is changing. Surface and sea temperatures are rising, the periodic cycle of seasons has been disturbed, the ozone layer is withering away, and the animal habitat is shrinking.

With such drastic changes happening in our planet, it is equally important to change our conventional ways of combating them.

Your task is to put forth an innovative solution to mitigate the effects of climate change.

Your idea can be anything ­ a product, a service, a business model. What matters is how much of an impact it will have in bringing about ‘Climate Unchange’.

Prizes worth Rs 15000/- * to be won.

Submissions for Screening round are open!

Please read the sections below to know the details and participate.

Rules
  • A team can comprise of a maximum of 2 members
  • In the Online round, you have to answer some questions through which you have to explain your idea
  • Based on the responses in this round, top 10 teams will be selected for the Finale to to held at IIT Guwahati during Reflux (March 25-27)
  • Entries for the first round have to be made till 21th March
Online screening round

You have to explain your idea by answering the following questions:

  • Give a brief (250 word) abstract of your idea
  • What are the existing problems that your idea addresses? Why is your idea important? (300 words)
  • Describe your idea in detail. (500 words)
  • What other existing solutions similar to yours are already in existence? (200 words)
  • How is your idea innovative and creative? (200 words)
  • How feasible is your idea, both economically and technologically? (250 words)
Besides, you need to upload an image depicting your idea. This can be a flowchart, model, anything that further helps in understanding your idea.

Judging:

Your idea will be judged on the basis of originality, creativity, technical and economic feasibility and relevance to the problem statement.

Top 10 teams will be invited to IIT Guwahati to present their idea during Reflux in presence of Panel of Industrialist and Professors.

Submission:
  • The answers to the above 6 questions have to be uploaded in the form of a word or PDF file
  • Download the word file with the questions written beforehand by clicking on the button given below
  • An image file of your idea also has to be uploaded as mentioned above
  • Follow the participation button below to upload these two files along with your details.

Onsite round

Top 10 teams will be invited to IIT Guwahati to present their idea during Reflux in presence of Panel of Industrialist and Professors.

The selected team will have to present a prototype of their idea by a physical model or by simulations or by a detailed analysis using animations and videos.
